My Story

My name is Fauziyat Kehinde Abdulfathai. I am from Kaduna State, Nigeria, and I am the first born in my family. I have a twin sister, a younger brother, and a younger sister.

I recently completed my Senior Secondary School education in 2026 at Victory School, Kakuri, and I wrote my WAEC at First Zenith School, Kakuri.

I am a very calm and reserved person. I don’t like partying or crowded places. I prefer staying indoors, where I can relax and enjoy peace. I love watching K-dramas, videos, and doing things that make me happy.

I also have a creative side, I am a tailor. I enjoy designing and creating fashion pieces whenever I have the opportunity.

I have a friend who calls me First Lady, a nickname that always makes me smile. It is just a fun name that shows our friendship and connection.

Even though I like fun and play sometimes, I also know when to be serious. I don’t tolerate nonsense, and I always try to do the right thing at the right time.
